Seed Round Investment Overview

Background

The Max Analytics journey started over a beer with one minor hockey association who shared our belief that the annual fall minor hockey evaluations should be used for more than just annual team formation. Quickly one association turned into two and in 2015 we embarked on a mission to build a software platform that streamlined the administration of hockey evaluations while delivering feedback in the form of a report card to kids. Max Analytics now serves 12 hockey associations in the greater Winnipeg area and has delivered over 15,000 report cards to athletes and their parents.

Over the last five years we focused our resources to ensure Max Analytics has a strong foundation for national and international growth. Specifically, Max Analytics is;

  • built for scale and mass adoption
  • easy to use
  • accurately and consistently collects data
  • accurately calculates and delivers results

Over the years we have also discovered that Max Analytics can be more than just a tool to help administer evaluations and deliver report cards to kids, but more so inform and guide athlete development, team formation, and league formation.

Future

Our vision is to become the leading sports software provider for athletes, coaches, and administrators around the world who are looking to track, compare, and foster meaningful competition and development.

To reach our goal, now is the time to accelerate our growth and we recognize that we alone do not have the resources to get there. So, today we are embarking on private equity campaign to raise $500,000 which will enable us to accelerate down our path towards our vision.

We are offing an exclusive investment opportunity to any** person who shares our belief that data informed athletes, coaches, and governing bodies can evolve minor sports around the world.

Qualified investors** who invest a minimum of $50,000 CAD will receive 2.5% of the company and Manitoba based investors will have the opportunity redeem a 45% non-refundable tax credit via the Small Business Venture Capital Tax Credit program.
