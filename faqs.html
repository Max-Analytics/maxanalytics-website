Common Questions

See the answers to some frequently asked questions below

  • How do I get set up?

    We'll start with a call to understand and align with the unique needs of your evaluations. Once we've defined the scope of the evaluation then we will guide you through the set up and execution of your evaluation from beginning to end.

  • Can I use my own evaluation format?

    You can use a custom evaluation format in place of the standard option. Speak to your account manager about setting this up.

  • Do I need my own devices?

    If you would like to score players on-ice, you will need a tablet or a mobile phone. Max Analytics does not provide devices at this time.

  • What does the software look like?

    Feel free to schedule a demo with us and we would be happy to show you. No commitment necessary.

  • Are all of the features mandatory?

    Everything we offer is optional and can be turned off. Please let your account manager know if there are features you would like disabled.
